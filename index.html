This website is for you, nature, and your nature

Life is increasingly becoming more distanced from nature. We have peloton bikes, surround sound systems, virtual reality, Pokemon Go, that makes us feel like we are outside, but we aren't getting the full effects of nature. This website is here to make life, getting into nature, and preserving nature a little easier.
