This website is for you, nature, and your nature
Life is increasingly becoming more distanced from nature. We have peloton bikes, surround sound systems, virtual reality, and Pokemon Go that make us feel like we are outside, but we aren't getting the full effects of nature. This website is here to make life, getting into nature, and preserving nature a little easier.
Complexity of Nature

Nature can be enjoyed in the rugged vastness of a grass field in Africa.

Nature can be enjoyed in the stillness of a mysterious waterfall in the depths of a forest.

Nature can be enjoyed in the midst of crashing, turquoise waters on a sunny day.

Nature can be enjoyed in the activity of a city park.

In every walk with nature, one receives far more than he seeks.
- John Muir
